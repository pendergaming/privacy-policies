Privacy Policy for Evo: Your AI Companion

Last Updated: January 5, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Evo: Your AI Companion mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices that was created by Pender Gaming (hereby referred to as "Service Provider" or "we", "us", "our") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Evo: Your AI Companion is a personal AI companion app that evolves based on your conversations. Your Evo develops unique personality traits, learns from your interactions, and provides companionship through AI-powered conversations. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information.

Information Collection and Use

Evo: Your AI Companion is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection and store most information locally on your device.

Personal Information We Collect

  • User-Generated Content: Your Evo's name, conversation history with your companion, and any custom settings you configure.
  • Companion Data: Your Evo's personality traits, energy levels, evolution history, visual customizations, and statistics.
  • Inventory Data: Virtual currency balance (coins), owned cosmetic items, and purchase history for in-app items.
  • API Configuration: If you configure your own AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Cohere, or Ollama), your API keys are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage. We never have access to your API keys.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
Privacy First: All conversation data and companion information is stored LOCALLY on your device. We do not have access to your conversations, your Evo's personality, or any personal data unless you choose to use external AI services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Companion Experience: Generate your Evo's personality traits, visual appearance, and responses based on your interactions
  • Local Storage: Save your companion's state, conversation history, and progress on your device
  • App Functionality: Maintain settings, preferences, and customizations you select
  • Monetization: Track virtual currency (coins) earned and spent within the app
  • Home Screen Widget: Display your Evo's current status on your device's home screen

Data Storage and Security

Local Device Storage

Evo: Your AI Companion stores data locally on your device using:

  • SharedPreferences: Stores your Evo's state, conversation history, personality data, inventory, and app settings
  • Secure Storage (flutter_secure_storage): Encrypts and stores your API keys if you configure external AI services

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall the app, all local data is deleted.

Memory Tiers

  • Free Tier: Conversation messages older than 24 hours are automatically deleted from your device to manage storage. Your Evo's personality and stats are always retained.
  • Evo Plus (Premium): Permanent message storage - all conversations are kept indefinitely on your device.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) Usage

Evo: Your AI Companion uses AI services to generate your companion's conversational responses. Here's how it works:

Your Choice of AI Provider

You can configure the app to use one of the following AI services:

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): Requires your own OpenAI API key
  • Anthropic (Claude): Requires your own Anthropic API key
  • Google (Gemini): Requires your own Google AI API key
  • Cohere: Requires your own Cohere API key
  • Ollama (Self-Hosted): Connects to your own local AI server
  • Mock AI (Default): Uses pre-generated responses without any external service
Important: When you use external AI services, your conversations are sent to the respective AI provider according to YOUR API key. We do not control or have access to this data. Please review the privacy policies of your chosen AI provider.

What Data is Sent to AI Services

When you configure an external AI provider, the following data is sent:

  • Your message text
  • Your Evo's personality traits (to generate context-appropriate responses)
  • Recent conversation history (to maintain context)

What is NOT Sent to AI Services

  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Other personal information
  • Data about other users

AI Provider Privacy Policies

If you use external AI services, please review their privacy policies:

  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google AI: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms
  • Cohere: https://cohere.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Rewarded Ads

You can optionally watch rewarded video ads to earn virtual currency (coins) within the app. Watching ads is entirely voluntary and not required to use the app.

Home Screen Widget

Evo: Your AI Companion offers an optional Android home screen widget that displays your companion's status. The widget:

  • Displays data stored locally on your device
  • Shows your Evo's name, current emotion, energy level, and coin balance
  • Does not transmit any data off your device
  • Can be removed at any time through your device's widget settings

Data Retention

Data retention in Evo: Your AI Companion:

  • Companion Data: Stored locally until you delete the app or reset your companion
  • Conversation History (Free Tier): Messages older than 24 hours are automatically deleted
  • Conversation History (Evo Plus): Messages are retained permanently on your device
  • API Keys: Stored in encrypted secure storage until you remove them or uninstall the app
  • Settings and Preferences: Retained until you uninstall the app

Your Rights

Since Evo: Your AI Companion stores all data locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is stored on your device and accessible through the app
  • Correction: You can modify your Evo's name and settings at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete all data through the app's Settings > Reset Evo feature, or by uninstalling the app
  • Data Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats on your device
  • Control: You control which AI service (if any) you connect to

How to Delete Your Data

To delete all your Evo companion data:

  1. Open Evo: Your AI Companion
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to "Reset App"
  4. Tap "Reset Everything"
  5. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data from your device.

No Account Required

Evo: Your AI Companion does not require you to create an account. All data is stored locally on your device, which means:

  • No registration or login required
  • No cloud synchronization (data stays on your device)
  • No server-side data collection
  • Complete privacy and control over your data

Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • API keys are stored using flutter_secure_storage with encryption
  • Conversations are stored locally and not transmitted to our servers
  • Connections to external AI services use HTTPS encryption
  • No cloud storage or remote data collection

However, please note that the security of your data also depends on your device's security settings and protections.
