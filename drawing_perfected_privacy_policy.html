Privacy Policy for Drawing Perfected

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Drawing Perfected mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices that was created by our development team (hereby referred to as "Service Provider" or "we", "us", "our") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe creative experience.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what is required for basic app functionality
  • Your drawings or artwork
  • Photos from your camera or gallery (they remain on your device)
  • Payment or financial information

Local Data Storage

The Application stores the following data locally on your device:

  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences (such as color choices, brush sizes, and drawing tool preferences) are saved locally using SharedPreferences to provide a consistent experience across sessions.
  • Cached Images: When you select images from online sources (Art Institute of Chicago or Met Museum APIs), they are temporarily processed on your device to create outlines. These images are not stored permanently or transmitted to any servers.
  • All of this data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Device Permissions

The Application requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera: To allow you to take photos that can be converted into drawing outlines. Photos are processed locally and not uploaded anywhere.
  • Photo Library/Gallery: To allow you to select existing photos from your device to trace. Selected photos remain on your device and are not uploaded.
  • Internet: To fetch artwork images from the Art Institute of Chicago and Metropolitan Museum of Art public APIs, and to display advertisements.
  • Storage: To save your completed drawings to your device's gallery when you choose to save them.

All permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and no data from these permissions is transmitted to third parties except as described in the Third-Party Services section below.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Art Institute of Chicago API

The Application fetches public domain artwork images from the Art Institute of Chicago's public API to provide sketch and drawing references. When you select "Art Sketches & Drawings," the app requests images from their servers. This API may log basic request information (IP address, request time) as part of normal server operation. Please refer to the Art Institute of Chicago's terms of use for more information.

Metropolitan Museum of Art API

The Application fetches public domain artwork images from the Metropolitan Museum of Art's public API to provide masterpiece references. When you select "Museum Masterpieces," the app requests images from their servers. This API may log basic request information (IP address, request time) as part of normal server operation. Please refer to the Met Museum's terms and conditions for more information.

Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The Application does not require any registration or account creation. Parents and guardians should be aware that the app:

  • Displays advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Accesses the device camera and photo library (only when explicitly granted permission)
  • Downloads artwork images from public museum APIs

We encourage parents to monitor their children's app usage. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with any personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Security

We value your trust in using our Application. Since we do not collect or transmit any personal information, there is minimal security risk. Your photos, drawings, and preferences remain exclusively on your device and are protected by your device's security measures. When saving drawings to your gallery, they are stored using your device's standard photo storage mechanisms.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our servers. Your locally stored data (preferences and temporary cached images) can be cleared by:

  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the Application
  • Clearing the Application's data in your device settings

Your saved drawings are stored in your device's photo gallery and can be managed through your device's photo management features.

Data Retention

The Application stores your preferences locally on your device indefinitely until you clear the app data or uninstall the application. Temporary image data is automatically cleared when you exit the app or select a new image. Your saved drawings are stored in your device's photo gallery until you choose to delete them. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties (except as described in the Third-Party Services section above).

International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no international data transfers of your information. However:

  • Google AdMob may transfer data internationally as part of their advertising services
  • The Art Institute of Chicago and Metropolitan Museum APIs are hosted in the United States, and requests to these services originate from your device

Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these services for more information about their data practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

This privacy policy page was created to ensure transparency about data practices in Drawing Perfected and to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Consent

By using our Application, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
