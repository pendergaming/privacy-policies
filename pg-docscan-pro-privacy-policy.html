PG DocScan Pro - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2024

Introduction

PG DocScan Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is a document scanning application developed by Pender Legacy Holdings LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Camera and Photos

The app requires access to your device's camera to scan documents. Photos taken within the app are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. You may also import images from your photo library for scanning purposes.

Locally Stored Data

All scanned documents, PDFs, and images are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your scanned documents, and they are never uploaded to any external servers.

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect certain information for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data
  • Advertising ID

On iOS devices, you will be asked for permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can choose to allow or deny this tracking.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for:

  • Providing document scanning functionality
  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Improving the app's performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your scanned documents are stored only on your device. We do not maintain any servers that store your personal documents. The security of your documents depends on your device's security settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your locally stored documents at any time through the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Deny tracking permission on iOS devices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
