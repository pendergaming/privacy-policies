Privacy Policy for Six-Minute Saga

Last Updated: January 5, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Six-Minute Saga mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices that was created by Pender Gaming (hereby referred to as "Service Provider" or "we", "us", "our") as a free service with optional in-app purchases. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Six-Minute Saga is an AI-powered interactive story generator that creates unique narrative adventures based on your choices. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information.

Information Collection and Use

Six-Minute Saga collects certain information to provide and improve our services and to enable the AI-powered story generation features.

Personal Information We Collect

  • Game Progress Data: Your story credits, saga library, completed chapters, and choices made during gameplay.
  • Purchase History: Records of credit packs and theme pack purchases (processed by Google Play).
  • Theme Pack Ownership: Which theme packs (Sci-Fi, Horror, History) you have purchased.
  • Usage Statistics: Daily bonus claims, ad views, and app engagement metrics.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and analytics.
  • Preferences: Your saved settings and preferences within the app.
No Personal Identification Required: Six-Minute Saga does not require you to create an account or provide any personally identifiable information such as name, email, or phone number to play the game.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Story Generation: Create unique AI-generated narratives based on your character, setting, and goal choices
  • Game Progress: Save your saga library, credit balance, and gameplay progress locally on your device
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Understand how players use the app to improve gameplay experience
  • Monetization: Process in-app purchases and deliver rewarded ad credits
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and resolve technical issues

Data Storage

Local Device Storage

The Application stores most data locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Your saga library (completed stories)
  • Credit balance and purchase history
  • Theme pack ownership
  • Daily bonus claim timestamps
  • App preferences and settings

Important: If you uninstall the app, this local data will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

Firebase Cloud Services

Six-Minute Saga uses Google Firebase for analytics and remote configuration:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the game
  • Firebase Remote Config: Allows us to adjust game parameters (credit prices, daily bonuses) without requiring app updates

All Firebase data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest using Google's security infrastructure. Firebase complies with major privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

For more information about Firebase's security practices, visit: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Artificial Intelligence (AI) Usage

Six-Minute Saga uses Google's Gemini AI to generate unique story content. Here's how it works:

  • What We Send: Your selected character, setting, goal, previous story text, and your choice from the last chapter
  • What We Receive: AI-generated story chapters (approximately 100 words) and two choices for the next chapter
  • Privacy: No personally identifiable information is sent to the AI service. Only game-related context is shared.
  • Data Retention: According to Google's API terms, prompts and outputs may be retained for up to 24 hours for abuse detection, but are not used for model training

For more information about Google's AI data practices, visit: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms

Image Generation Services (Optional)

Story images are generated using third-party AI image generation services:

  • Replicate.com (Optional): When enabled, creature images are generated via Replicate's Stable Diffusion API
  • Default Placeholders: If image generation is disabled, stylized placeholder images are used instead

Image generation prompts contain only story descriptions and no personal information. These services may cache generated images according to their terms of service.

Third-Party Services

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application. AdMob may collect certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Google Play In-App Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play's billing system. Google Play may collect:

  • Payment information (credit card, payment method)
  • Purchase history
  • Google account information

We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Google Play.

Google Play's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Six-Minute Saga is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has used the app, please contact us at pendergamification@gmail.com so that we can take necessary action.

Parents should be aware that the game:

  • Uses AI to generate story content (may produce unpredictable content)
  • Displays advertisements
  • Includes in-app purchases
  • Does not require account creation or personal information

Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Local Game Data: Stored on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics are retained by Firebase for up to 14 months
  • Purchase Records: Google Play retains purchase history according to their policies
  • AI-Generated Content: Story text and choices are stored locally on your device. Google's AI service may retain prompts for up to 24 hours for abuse prevention

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request information about data we process
  • Deletion: Clear all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in device settings
  • Data Portability: Export your saga library by taking screenshots or using device backup features
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Disable analytics in the app settings
  • Opt-Out of Ads: Use your device's ad personalization settings

How to Delete Your Data

To delete all data associated with Six-Minute Saga:

  1. Go to your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps > Six-Minute Saga
  3. Select "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"
  4. Optionally uninstall the app

For questions about data deletion, contact us at pendergamification@gmail.com

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Google Firebase and Google Cloud services have data centers worldwide and comply with international data transfer regulations including:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • GDPR requirements for international transfers

Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmissions to our servers are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Local data on your device is protected by your device's security features
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information
  • Firebase security rules restrict unauthorized data access
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
