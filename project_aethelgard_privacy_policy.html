Privacy Policy for Project Aethelgard

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Project Aethelgard mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application", "App", "Game" or "Service") for mobile devices that was created by Pender Gaming (hereby referred to as "Service Provider", "we", "us", "our"). This service is provided as a freemium game with optional in-app purchases and advertising.

Project Aethelgard is an AI-powered Dungeons & Dragons-style role-playing game that uses artificial intelligence to create dynamic storytelling experiences and supports both single-player and multiplayer game sessions.

Important: This Application collects and stores certain personal information to provide multiplayer functionality, save game progress across devices, and deliver AI-powered gameplay experiences. Please read this policy carefully to understand how your data is collected, used, and protected.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects and processes various types of information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including account management, multiplayer functionality, game progression, and AI-driven content generation.

Account Information

To use the Application, you must create an account. We collect and store:

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and account recovery
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored for account security (we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Display Name: Your chosen username displayed to other players in multiplayer sessions
  • User ID: A unique identifier automatically generated by our authentication system

Alternative Sign-In Methods

We offer the following third-party authentication options:

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We do not have access to your Google password.
  • Apple Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive the information you authorize Apple to share, which may include your name and email address (or a private relay email).

Game Data and Progress

We collect and store the following game-related information in our Firebase Cloud Firestore database:

  • Character Data: Your created characters, including names, races, classes, levels, stats, abilities, equipment, and inventory
  • Game Sessions: Active and saved game sessions, including session IDs, game codes, party composition, quest progress, and current game state
  • Quest Progress: Your completed objectives, active quests, rewards earned, and story progression
  • Chat History: In-game narrative text, player messages, and AI-generated story content within game sessions
  • Combat Data: Turn order, enemy encounters, combat actions, and battle outcomes
  • Party Resources: Shared inventory, gold, experience points, and items within game sessions
  • Game Preferences: Your selected game mode (solo or multiplayer) and gameplay settings

In-App Purchase Data

If you make purchases within the Application, we store:

  • Purchase History: Records of what items, races, classes, or currency you've purchased
  • Fate Token Balance: Your current balance of in-game premium currency
  • Gold Balance: Your current balance of in-game standard currency
  • Unlocked Content: Premium races, classes, AI companions, and cosmetic items you've purchased
  • Ad-Free Status: Whether you've purchased ad removal

Note: We do NOT store credit card numbers, billing addresses, or other payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play's billing system or Apple's App Store.

Multiplayer and Social Data

When you participate in multiplayer sessions, we collect:

  • Game Codes: Shareable codes used to join multiplayer sessions
  • Player Lists: User IDs of players in your game sessions
  • In-Game Communication: Messages and actions you send during gameplay
  • Session Timestamps: When game sessions are created, joined, and last updated

AI-Generated Content

Project Aethelgard uses Google's Gemini AI to generate dynamic game content. When you play:

  • Prompts Sent to AI: We send game state information (party composition, character details, quest context, player actions) to Google's Gemini API to generate quests, narrative text, combat descriptions, and NPC dialogue
  • AI Responses: Generated content is stored in your game session within our Firebase database
  • No Personal Information in AI Prompts: We only send gameplay-related information to the AI service, not your email, real name, or other personal identifiers

For information about how Google processes AI requests, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Technical and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Crash Reports: Diagnostic information when the app encounters errors
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics to identify and fix technical issues
  • Usage Analytics: Which features you use and how often (collected via Firebase Analytics)

Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

Firebase Services (Google)

We use the following Firebase services provided by Google:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts and authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores game data, character information, and multiplayer session data
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage statistics to improve the game

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini AI (via Google Generative AI API) to generate dynamic game content including quests, narrative text, and dialogue. Game-related information is sent to Google's servers for processing.

Google AI Privacy Information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We display advertisements using Google AdMob (unless you purchase ad removal). AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Ad viewing and interaction data

You can opt-out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Google Play Services / Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed by:

  • Google Play Billing: For Android devices
  • Apple In-App Purchase: For iOS devices

We do not process payments directly or store payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Create, maintain, and secure your account
  • Game Functionality: Save your progress, characters, and game state across devices
  • Multiplayer Features: Enable you to create, join, and participate in multiplayer game sessions
  • AI Content Generation: Generate personalized quests, narratives, and gameplay experiences
  • In-App Purchases: Process and track your purchases of premium content
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and resolve technical issues
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve game features and fix bugs
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms of service

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Other Players: Your display name and in-game actions are visible to other players in multiplayer sessions you join
  • Service Providers: We share data with Firebase, Google Cloud, and AdMob as necessary to provide game functionality
  • AI Processing: Game context (but not personal information) is sent to Google Gemini AI for content generation
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: If Pender Gaming is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the new entity

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the service:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Game Progress: Stored indefinitely to preserve your characters and saved games
  • Chat History: Stored within game sessions for the lifetime of the session
  • Purchase Records: Retained for at least 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and aggregated data may be retained indefinitely

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Firebase Firestore encrypts all stored data by default
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage (we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to our database and systems
  • Security Rules: Firebase security rules prevent unauthorized access to game data

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
