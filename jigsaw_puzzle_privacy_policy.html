Privacy Policy for Jigsaw Puzzle

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Jigsaw Puzzle mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices that was created by our development team (hereby referred to as "Service Provider" or "we", "us", "our") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what is required for basic app functionality
  • User-generated content or communications
  • Payment or financial information
  • Your puzzle images (all images remain exclusively on your device)

Local Data Storage

The Application stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Saved Game States: Your in-progress puzzles including piece positions, connections, and drawer organization are saved locally to allow you to continue puzzles across sessions.
  • Puzzle Images: When you select an image to create a puzzle, the image and its puzzle pieces are processed and stored locally on your device. Images are never uploaded to any servers.
  • Game Preferences: Your preferred difficulty level (Easy, Hard, or Expert) and other settings are saved locally to provide a consistent experience.
  • Drawer Organization: Custom drawer names and piece organization preferences are stored locally.
  • All of this data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Image Processing

The Application allows you to create jigsaw puzzles from your own photos. This image processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Images are read from your device's photo library with your permission
  • The image is sliced into puzzle pieces on your device
  • Color analysis for piece filtering occurs locally on your device
  • No images or image data are ever transmitted off your device
  • Images and puzzle data can be deleted at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Permissions

The Application requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage/Photos Access: Required to allow you to select images from your photo library to create puzzles. We only read the images you explicitly select and never access other photos or files.
  • Internet Access: Required to display advertisements through Google AdMob. No other data is transmitted.

Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The Application does not require any registration or account creation. Parents and guardians should be aware that the app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. We encourage parents to monitor their children's app usage.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with any personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Security

We value your trust in using our Application. Since we do not collect or transmit any personal information, there is minimal security risk. The only data stored (your puzzle game states, images, and preferences) remains exclusively on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your locally stored puzzle data (saved games, images, and preferences) can be cleared by:

  • Using the "Delete Game" option within the app for individual saved puzzles
  • Clearing the Application's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the Application

Data Retention

The Application stores your puzzle game states, images, and preferences locally on your device indefinitely until you clear the app data, delete individual saved games, or uninstall the application. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties (except as described in the Third-Party Services section above).

International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no international data transfers of your information. However, Google AdMob may transfer data internationally as part of their advertising services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

This privacy policy page was created to ensure transparency about data practices in Jigsaw Puzzle and to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Consent
