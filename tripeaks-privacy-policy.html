Privacy Policy for TriPeaks Solitaire

Last updated: January 2025

Information Collection and Use

TriPeaks Solitaire is a simple card game that does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

Game Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your current win streak
  • Your best win streak

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Advertising

This app displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information for advertising purposes. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required to display advertisements
  • Access Network State: Required to check network connectivity for advertisements

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
