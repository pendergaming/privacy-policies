Privacy Policy for Box Tally

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Box Tally mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices that was created by our development team (hereby referred to as "Service Provider" or "we", "us", "our") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application uses Firebase services to enable core functionality including user authentication and data synchronization. We collect and process the following information:

What We Collect

  • Anonymous User ID: When you use the app, we create an anonymous identifier to associate your data with your account.
  • Display Name: The name you choose to identify yourself within the app.
  • Box and Item Data: Information about boxes and items you create, including names, descriptions, locations, categories, and estimated values.
  • Household Data: Information about households you create or join, including the household name and member list.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses (we use anonymous authentication)
  • Physical location/GPS data
  • Photos from your camera roll (unless you explicitly upload item photos)
  • Contacts or address book
  • Payment or financial information

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore, a secure cloud database service. This allows you to:

  • Access your box inventory from multiple devices
  • Share your household with family members
  • Keep your data synchronized in real-time

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

Google Firebase

We use Firebase for authentication and data storage. Firebase may collect:

  • Device information for analytics
  • App performance data
  • Crash reports to help us improve the app

For more information, please review Firebase's Privacy Policy at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application, including banner ads and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track

Rewarded Advertisements

The Application offers optional rewarded video advertisements that, when watched, provide additional storage capacity (more boxes or items per box). Viewing these ads is entirely voluntary. The rewarded ads are provided by Google AdMob and are subject to their privacy policies.

Data Sharing

Your box and item data may be shared with other members of your household if you choose to invite them using your household's share code. This is an intentional feature to allow families to collaborate on packing and moving.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as described in this policy.

Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Security

We value your trust in using our Application. Your data is protected by Firebase's security infrastructure, which includes encryption in transit and at rest. Access to your household data is restricted to authenticated members only.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. If you wish to delete your data, you can:

  • Leave a household (removes your membership)
  • Contact us to request account deletion

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Correct inaccurate data by editing your profile or items
  • Delete your data by leaving households or requesting account deletion
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

This privacy policy page was created to ensure transparency about data practices in Box Tally and to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Consent

By using our Application, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
