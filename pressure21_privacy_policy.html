This application is for entertainment purposes only.

  • No Real Money: Pressure21 does not involve real money gambling, betting, or wagering. All chips and currency used in the game are virtual and have no real-world monetary value.
  • Not a Gambling Service: This is a simulation game designed for entertainment. No real prizes, money, or anything of value can be won.

  • Responsible Gaming: The simulated gambling mechanics in this game are purely for entertainment and should not encourage real-world gambling behavior.
  • No Purchase Required: The game does not offer in-app purchases of virtual chips or currency.

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Pressure21 mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices that was created by our development team (hereby referred to as "Service Provider" or "we", "us", "our") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Pressure21 is a card game that simulates blackjack-style gameplay with a unique twist where the bust limit decreases with each card drawn. All gameplay uses virtual chips with no real-world value.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what is required for basic app functionality
  • User-generated content or communications
  • Payment or financial information
  • Gambling history or betting patterns
  • Any data related to your gameplay that could be used to identify you

Local Data Storage

The Application stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Virtual Chip Balance: Your current virtual chip balance is saved locally using SharedPreferences to maintain your progress between game sessions. These chips have no real-world monetary value.
  • Game Preferences: Your preference settings are saved to provide a consistent experience across game sessions.
  • All of this data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The Application does not require any registration or account creation.

Parental Guidance Recommended

While Pressure21 uses only virtual currency and involves no real money gambling, the game simulates casino-style card gameplay. We recommend that parents and guardians:

  • Be aware of the simulated gambling mechanics in the game
  • Discuss with children that this is entertainment only and does not involve real money
  • Monitor their children's app usage
  • Understand that the app displays advertisements through Google AdMob

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with any personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Security

We value your trust in using our Application. Since we do not collect or transmit any personal information, there is minimal security risk. The only data stored (your virtual chip balance and preferences) remains exclusively on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

No Real Money Transactions

Pressure21 does not process, collect, or store any payment information. There are no in-app purchases, and no real money can be wagered, won, or lost in the game. All chips and currency are virtual and for entertainment purposes only.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your locally stored game data (virtual chip balance and preferences) can be cleared by:

  • Using the "Reset Game" option in the app's menu
  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the Application
  • Clearing the Application's data in your device settings

Data Retention

The Application stores your virtual chip balance and preferences locally on your device indefinitely until you reset the game, clear the app data, or uninstall the application. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties (except as described in the Third-Party Services section above).

International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no international data transfers of your information. However, Google AdMob may transfer data internationally as part of their advertising services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Responsible Gaming

While Pressure21 is purely an entertainment application with no real-money gambling:

  • Remember that real gambling involves financial risk and this game does not prepare players for such risks
