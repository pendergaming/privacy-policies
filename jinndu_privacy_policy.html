Privacy Policy for Jinndu: The Shaper's Duel

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Jinndu: The Shaper's Duel mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application", "App", "Game" or "Service") for mobile devices that was created by Pender Gaming (hereby referred to as "Service Provider", "we", "us", "our"). This service is provided as a freemium game with optional in-app purchases and advertising.

Jinndu is a strategic 1v1 tactical card game where players take the role of powerful Genies who can shapeshift into various animal forms, manipulate environments, and cast powerful abilities in epic duels.

Important: This Application collects minimal personal information. Game progress is stored locally on your device. Advertising and in-app purchase services may collect certain data as described in this policy.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects limited information to provide game functionality and support monetization through ads and in-app purchases.

Game Data (Stored Locally)

The following game data is stored locally on your device using Shared Preferences:

  • Game Statistics: Win/loss records, games played, highest win streak
  • Game Settings: Sound preferences, tutorial completion status
  • Collection Progress: Which animal cards you've unlocked through gameplay or purchase
  • Premium Status: Whether you've purchased ad removal or other premium features

Note: This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall the app, this data will be deleted.

In-App Purchase Data

If you make purchases within the Application, we store locally:

  • Purchase Status: Records of which animal card packs or features you've purchased
  • Unlocked Content: Which premium animal cards and abilities you own
  • Ad-Free Status: Whether you've purchased ad removal

Note: We do NOT store credit card numbers, billing addresses, or other payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play's billing system or Apple's App Store.

Technical and Usage Data

We do not collect analytics or usage data. The app functions entirely offline except for advertising and in-app purchase services.

Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We display advertisements using Google AdMob (unless you purchase ad removal). AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data
  • Ad viewing and interaction data

You can opt-out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Google Play Services / Apple App Store

In-app purchases are processed by:

  • Google Play Billing: For Android devices
  • Apple In-App Purchase: For iOS devices

We do not process payments directly or store payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: Save your progress, settings, and unlocked cards locally on your device
  • In-App Purchases: Track which animal cards and features you've purchased
  • Advertising: Display ads unless you've purchased ad removal

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT collect or sell your personal information. However, third-party services integrated into the app may collect data:

  • AdMob: Collects advertising-related data as described in their privacy policy
  • Google Play / App Store: Processes payment information for in-app purchases

Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device:

  • Game Progress: Stored until you clear app data or uninstall the app
  • Purchase Records: Validated through Google Play / App Store when you restore purchases

Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's operating system security. We do not transmit game progress or personal information to external servers (except for ad serving and payment processing handled by Google/Apple).

Your Rights and Choices

Deletion

  • Clear Game Data: Use your device settings to clear app data or cache
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game data
  • Note: Purchase records remain with Google Play / App Store and can be restored if you reinstall

Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can disable personalized advertising in your device settings as described in the "Google Mobile Ads" section above, or purchase ad removal through the in-app store.

Children's Privacy

Jinndu: The Shaper's Duel is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Contains fantasy card game content suitable for all ages
  • Contains in-app purchases
  • Contains advertising (unless removed via purchase)

Parents should be aware that the app includes:

  • In-app purchases that require payment
  • Advertisements (can be removed via purchase)

International Data Transfers

Your game data is stored locally on your device. However, advertising data may be transferred to:

  • United States: Where Google AdMob servers are primarily located
  • Other Countries: Where Google maintains data centers for AdMob services

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Jinndu does not collect, store, or sell personal information. Game data is stored locally on your device. Advertising data is collected by Google AdMob - please see AdMob's privacy policy for details about your California privacy rights regarding advertising data.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

Jinndu does not collect or process personal data beyond what is required for advertising (handled by Google AdMob) and in-app purchases (handled by Google Play / Apple). For advertising data rights, please consult Google AdMob's privacy policy.
