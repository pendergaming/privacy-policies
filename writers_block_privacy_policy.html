Privacy Policy for Writer's Block

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Writer's Block ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: Writer's Block is a creative writing prompt generator. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the creative ideas you generate.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Generated Ideas: Writing prompts and creative ideas you generate using the app
  • Favorites: Ideas you mark as favorites for later reference
  • Generation History: Previous writing prompts you've created
  • App Preferences: Theme settings (dark/light mode) and other customizations

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by Google Mobile Ads for serving advertisements

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Storage Only

Your generated ideas, favorites, and history are stored ONLY on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or store your creative content on any remote servers.

2.2 Purpose of Data Use

  • To provide writing prompt generation functionality
  • To save your generated ideas, favorites, and history locally on your device
  • To remember your theme preferences
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To display advertisements through Google Mobile Ads

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All generated ideas, favorites, and history are stored locally on your device
  • App preferences are stored using Android's SharedPreferences
  • Your creative content remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers

3.2 Security Measures

  • We use Android's secure storage mechanisms to protect your data
  • Your creative ideas are accessible only through the app on your device
  • We do not have access to your generated content or favorites

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Mobile Ads

We use Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements in the app. Google may collect and use data according to their privacy policy:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage information

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, or disclose your generated ideas, favorites, or history to any third parties.

We may share limited, non-sensitive information in the following cases:

  • With Google for advertising purposes (as described above)
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Data Control

  • Access: You can view all your generated ideas and favorites within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual ideas or clear all history from the app settings
  • Export: You can share your generated ideas using the built-in share functionality

6.2 Advertising Opt-Out

You can limit ad tracking by:

  • Using your device's "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting
  • Visiting Google's ad settings: adssettings.google.com

7. Children's Privacy

Writer's Block is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 beyond what is necessary for app functionality. All data is stored locally on the device.

8. Data Retention

  • Generated ideas, favorites, and history are retained on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device
  • Analytics data may be retained for up to 26 months as per Google's policies

9. International Data Transfers

Your generated content is stored locally on your device and is not transferred internationally. However, advertising data may be processed by Google in various countries where they operate.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying an in-app notification for material changes

11. Your Consent

By using Writer's Block, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
