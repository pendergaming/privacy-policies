Privacy Policy for Hearts

Last updated: October 17, 2025

Introduction

Pendevogel Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Hearts mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of the App.

Information Collection and Use

Hearts is designed with your privacy in mind. The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Game Data

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Game statistics and scores
  • User preferences and settings
  • Game progress and state

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, etc.)
  • General location information (country/city level)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's Privacy Information.

Advertising Controls

You can control personalized advertising on your device through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game is designed to be family-friendly and safe for all ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Security

Since we do not collect personal information, there is minimal security risk. Game data stored on your device is protected by your device's security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to display advertisements
  • Network State: Used to check network connectivity for ads

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data through your device settings.

International Users

The App can be used worldwide. Advertisement data processing by Google AdMob may involve transferring data to countries outside your residence. Google maintains appropriate safeguards for international data transfers as described in their privacy policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our side. All game data on your device can be managed through your device settings.
