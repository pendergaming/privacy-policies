Privacy Policy for Book of Mormon Baseball

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Book of Mormon Baseball ("we", "our", or "the app") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing an enjoyable gaming and educational experience.

2. Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal information necessary for game functionality:

  • Game Data: Game statistics, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising Data: Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information, advertising identifiers, and interaction data to serve personalized ads. This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide core game functionality
  • Save your game progress and preferences locally
  • Display relevant advertisements through AdMob
  • Improve game performance and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect and use data according to their privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Information

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (Ad Settings on Android, or Limit Ad Tracking on iOS).

5. Data Storage

All game data (preferences, statistics, and game state) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. If you uninstall the app, this local data will be deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as a general audience educational trivia game. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. Since game data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for securing your device with appropriate passwords or biometric authentication.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored game data through the app interface
  • Delete your data by clearing app data in device settings or uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Users

This app is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this policy, regardless of your location.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the app's support channel or app store listing.

12. Consent

By using Book of Mormon Baseball, you consent to this privacy policy and agree to its terms.
