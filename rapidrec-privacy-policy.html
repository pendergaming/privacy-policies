Privacy Policy for RapidRec

Last Updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how RapidRec ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: RapidRec is a screen recording and video trimming application. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your recordings and personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Screen Recordings: Video recordings you create using the app
  • Audio Data: Microphone audio captured during recordings (when enabled)
  • Trimmed Videos: Edited video files you create using our video trimmer
  • App Preferences: Settings and customizations you configure within the app

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by Google Mobile Ads for serving advertisements

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Storage Only

Your screen recordings, trimmed videos, and audio are stored ONLY on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or store your recordings on any remote servers.

2.2 Purpose of Data Use

  • To provide screen recording functionality
  • To enable video trimming and editing features
  • To save your recordings locally on your device
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To display advertisements

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All recordings are stored locally on your device
  • Your videos remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers
  • Trimmed and edited videos are saved to your device's storage or photo library

3.2 Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard practices for app development
  • All video data remains private and local to your device
  • We do not have access to your recordings or personal content

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Mobile Ads

We use Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements in the app. Google may collect and use data according to their privacy policy:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage information

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, we request your permission before allowing advertising networks to track your activity across apps. You can change this setting at any time in your device's Privacy settings.

5. Permissions We Request

5.1 Screen Recording Permission

Required to capture your screen. On iOS, this uses ReplayKit's system broadcast feature. On Android, this uses MediaProjection.

5.2 Microphone Permission

Optional permission to record audio from your microphone during screen recordings. You can disable this in the app settings.

5.3 Photo Library Permission

Required to save your recordings to your device's photo library for easy access and sharing.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, or disclose your recordings, videos, or personal content to any third parties.

We may share limited, non-sensitive information in the following cases:

  • With Google for advertising purposes (as described above)
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Data Control

  • Access: You can view all your recordings within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual recordings or all data from the app
  • Sharing: You control when and how to share your recordings

7.2 Advertising Preferences

  • On iOS, you can opt out of tracking via App Tracking Transparency
  • On Android, you can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in device settings

8. Children's Privacy

RapidRec is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

  • Recordings are retained on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored recordings
  • Analytics data may be retained for up to 26 months as per Google's policies

10. International Data Transfers

Your recordings remain on your device and are not transferred internationally. Only advertising-related data may be processed by Google's servers in various locations according to their privacy policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

12. Your Consent

By using RapidRec, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
