Privacy Policy for Tile Tango

Last Updated: November 1, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Tile Tango mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application" or "App") for mobile devices that was created by our development team (hereby referred to as "Service Provider" or "we", "us", "our") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what is required for basic app functionality
  • User-generated content or communications
  • Payment or financial information

Local Data Storage

The Application stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game Progress: Your current game state, including the board layout, player tiles, and turn information are saved locally using SharedPreferences to maintain your progress between game sessions.
  • Game Settings: Your preference settings and game options are saved to provide a consistent experience across game sessions.
  • All of this data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the Application. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The Application does not require any registration or account creation. Parents and guardians should be aware that the app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. We encourage parents to monitor their children's app usage.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with any personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Security

We value your trust in using our Application. Since we do not collect or transmit any personal information, there is minimal security risk. The only data stored (your game progress and preferences) remains exclusively on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your locally stored game data (progress and preferences) can be cleared by:

  • Uninstalling and reinstalling the Application
  • Clearing the Application's data in your device settings

Data Retention

The Application stores your game progress and preferences locally on your device indefinitely until you clear the app data or uninstall the application. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties (except as described in the Third-Party Services section above).

International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no international data transfers of your information. However, Google AdMob may transfer data internationally as part of their advertising services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

This privacy policy page was created to ensure transparency about data practices in Tile Tango and to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Consent

By using our Application, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
