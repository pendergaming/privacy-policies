Privacy Policy for FamHub

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

FamHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our family communication app.

Information We Collect

To provide our family communication services, we collect the following information:

Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Display name (chosen by you)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Family Data

  • Family group memberships
  • Messages sent within your family groups
  • Calendar events you create
  • Notes posted to the family fridge

Device Information

  • Device tokens for push notifications
  • App usage data for improving our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide family messaging and communication features
  • Send push notifications for messages and events
  • Sync your family calendar and shared content
  • Improve and maintain our services

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Messages and family data are only accessible to members of your family group.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your family messages and data are private to your family group. We may share data only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who help operate our app (e.g., Firebase, Google Cloud)

Advertising

This app displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain device information for advertising purposes. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

FamHub includes a Kid Mode feature designed for family use. Children should only use the app under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can manage their children's access through Kid Mode settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of push notifications

To exercise these rights, use the Settings menu in the app or contact us directly.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data is removed and your messages are anonymized to maintain conversation context for other family members.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
