Privacy Policy for Surveyor

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how Surveyor ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information.

Information Collection and Use

Surveyor is designed with your privacy in mind. The app stores only the following information locally on your device:

  • Game statistics (win streak, best streak)
  • Game preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Data Collection

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to display advertisements
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to check network connectivity for ad delivery

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted by the app itself.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is a simple card game suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.
